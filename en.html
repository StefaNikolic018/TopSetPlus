About us

What do we do

TopSet plus is a company whose activity is fishing production equipment, and the primary activity is the production of feeders. Many models of our feeders are lead free because we try to preserve the environment, everything it provides us as well as hers immeasurable beauty. If you are interested in cooperation, don't hesitate to contact us via social media, email or mobile phone.
